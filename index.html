NodeConf Barcelona is the Barcelona-edition of the longest running community driven conference for the Node community. After the very successful WeNode-conference last year we're back as "NodeConf Barcelona". Let's celebrate this year of exciting moments like the release of node.js v4.0.0 together with great talks and discussions.

Welcome to Barcelona!

A full day conference in the center of Barcelona including:

  • One full day conference
  • Single track
  • Talks ranging from beginners to expert levels
  • Coffee breaks and refreshments throughout the day
  • Mediterranean-style lunch menu served right on the venue
  • Talk to amazing people from all over Europe

How to reach the conference

Conference is held in the Mobile World Capital Barcelona building. Public transport stop is Plaça Catalunya for Metro (TMB), Train (FGC, RENFE) and bus (TMB, Aerobus, ...).

If you need information about hotels, flights or transportation you can mail us at: nodeconf@barcelonajs.org.
